cawtooth — AY-3-8910 chord

Boots Peter Sovietov's Ayumi emulator (AY-3-8910 / YM2149) in an AudioWorklet, programs all three tone channels with the notes of a chord, and triggers them simultaneously. This is the AY counterpart to the SID and OPL tone demos — proves the chip-emulator path through the new worklet end-to-end.
